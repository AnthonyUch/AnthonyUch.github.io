Modular Mayhem

A concept for a first-person shooter that borrows elements of survivors-like games. Features a procedurally generated maze that expands in real time.

You are placed within this maze and must survive a neverending wave of enemies that will try to ambush and surround you at every possible angle.

By killing enemies or destroying the large orbs scattered throughout the maze, you are able to gain experience points. Gathering enough will allow you to level up, unlocking variety of upgrades to choose from that will augment your weapon or character, from ricocheting bullets, to recharging forcefields.
