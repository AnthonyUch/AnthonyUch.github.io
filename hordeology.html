Hordeology

A first-person shooter prototype built in Unity, featuring physics-based movement, enemy AI, and item pickups.

My first game developed in Unity. A very simple game where the objective is to survive as long as possible.

The player is bombarded by increasing waves of enemies. Trapped in a warehouse, they're forced to leverage whatever they can to survive.

The player has a number of options to combat the oncoming wave, from beating them up with their fists to using the many boxes littered around. Boxes can be used as throwable projectiles or temporary blockades that will stop or slow down enemies. Destroyed boxes will also drop many useful pickups such as medkits and guns.

Controls

Punch / Shoot / Throw: Left Click

Grab / Drop: E

Movement: WASD

Jump: Space

Menu: Esc
